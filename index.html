Motivation

  • Plant diseases and pests directly threaten food production and production of plant-based materials
  • influence agricultural and industrial trade in an area after establishment of necessary quarantine zones
  • Understanding the potential spread of pests and pathogens is thus crucial for protecting the economy and food security
  • CGA working with USDA APHIS.
